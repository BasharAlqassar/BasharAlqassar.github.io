About Me


My name is Bashar Alqassar; I am a Graduate student studying Interactive Media and Game Development (IMGD) at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. I also hold a Bachlers of Science in Computer Science and a Bachlers of Arts in IMGD.

I am proficient in working in the Godot Game Engine using their custom GD-Script Language. I am also fluent in coding in Java, Javascript, Python, C/C++, C#, and HTML. I emphasize modular design whenever possible in anything I make and always code with the end-user experience in mind with the games I create.

On teams, I love working directly with other disciplines in a project to find the best ways of developing code systems that can elevate art, writing, design, or audio work to new heights, making good work become exceptional.
